About Us

We develop alloys and inorganic compounds of metals

StroLabs was established in Trabzon Teknokent in 2021 with the TÜBİTAK-1512 Entrepreneurship Support Program.

It is an R&D start-up that is developing valuable materials and alloys such as master alloys, HEAs, nano oxides, borides, and carbides. While carrying out these studies, it is aimed to develop economic and Eco-friendly processes by evaluating primary (ore) and secondary (scrap, waste, etc.) resources.
